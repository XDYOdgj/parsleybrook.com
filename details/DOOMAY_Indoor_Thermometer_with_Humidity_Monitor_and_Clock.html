DOOMAY Indoor Thermometer with Humidity Monitor and Clock

Stay in control of your indoor environment with this sleek and functional thermometer. It combines accurate temperature and humidity readings with a convenient date and clock feature, making it perfect for your home, office, or any space where environmental comfort matters.

Precision & Accuracy

This device features a high-precision sensor that delivers reliable readings every 10 seconds. Whether you're monitoring your living room or a specialized area like your guitar room, you can trust the accuracy to keep you informed and comfortable.

Easy to Use & Read

With its 3.62-inch LCD display, reading temperature and humidity is a breeze. The intuitive interface lets you switch between Celsius and Fahrenheit, and toggle between 12-hour and 24-hour formats with a single press.

Adaptable & Versatile

Designed for multiple environments, this thermometer offers versatile mounting options. It can be placed on a desk, mounted on a wall using the included adhesive pad, or attached to a magnetic surface for easy access and optimal visibility.

Comfort Indicator & Smart Adjustments

The comfort indicator display features face icons that quickly show you the current indoor condition. These icons help you understand whether you need to adjust your humidifier or dehumidifier, ensuring the perfect balance for your daily life.

Long-Lasting & Reliable

With a built-in date and clock function, this thermometer is not only a tracking tool but also a time-saving one. It comes with long battery life, ensuring it runs smoothly without frequent replacements.

Customizable for Your Needs

For added precision, the device supports manual calibration, adapting to any unique setting. It's the ideal choice for anyone who values control, accuracy, and an elegant design that fits seamlessly into your space.
